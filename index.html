Regional Connectivity: Opportunities for the Region

To promote regional cooperation and development, this initiative aims to foster dialogue and socio-economic integration among neighboring countries. It seeks to enhance business-to-business (B2B) and people-to-people (P2P) connections, promote multimodal connectivity, and highlight Pakistan’s strategic potential as a regional connectivity hub.
